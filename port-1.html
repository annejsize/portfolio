The Brain Dump

  • Client: UT Bootcamp
  • Skills: Fullstack
  • Type of Project: Group

This is a social media app, inspired by the likes of Facebook, Instagram, Reddit and so on. Within the app, user is prompted to register. Log-in information is saved to a database via MongoDB. Using Node/Express, based on user inputs, data is saved, updated and/or deleted, and routed to the appropriate pages. The main page is made up of the 'timeline screen', a la Facebook style, where user can post ideas, pictures or other files that they wish to share on the Internet. The user is also able to comment on other posts and either 'like' or 'dislike' them.

The catch? The post disappears after 24 hours unless it has accumlate enough likes - then the post lives on - or dislikes - the post then disappers.
